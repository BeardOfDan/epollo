Welcome to epollo!

What is it?

A free to use story sharing website where You can

  • Read stories from users
  • Write your own stories
  • Write new threads to others stories which are related to the original storyline
  • Have fun

How it works?

Simply. For uploading stories you must register first here and then

From to explore page select a right story for you or search for stories by users(authors) or title, and then you can add and write new story threads to those stories (every story and thread is threadable). So in the end a story is looks like a tree, where the branches are the threads and the tribe is the original story.

The stories or the threads you wrote will appear on your profile.

Or You can just read stories even without registration.
